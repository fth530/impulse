# Privacy Policy for Impulse

**Effective Date:** March 9, 2026

Thank you for choosing to play **Impulse - Tek Tuş Matematik** ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding data collection and usage.

## 1. Information We Collect
**We do not collect any personal data.** 
The App functions entirely on your device and does not require an account, email address, or any other personally identifiable information (PII) to operate.

### a. Local Data Storage
The App saves certain game data directly on your device (using local storage mechanisms like AsyncStorage):
- Your highest score and current streak.
- Total games played and other analytical statistics.
- App preferences such as sound and vibration settings.
**This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third-party databases.**

### b. Analytics and Diagnostics
We do not integrate any third-party tracking or analytics SDKs (e.g., Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel) that collect your data. Any crash reports, if generated by the operating system (like Apple crash reports), are strictly anonymous and opt-in per your iOS settings.

## 2. Third-Party Services
The App does not use any third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

## 3. Children’s Privacy
Our App is safe for users of all ages and complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect your contact information, we cannot notify you individually. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date".
