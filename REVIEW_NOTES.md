# App Store Review Notes

Copy this text into App Store Connect → App Review Information → Notes

---

Thank you for reviewing our app.

MathPulse is a single-tap mental math reflex game designed for quick brain training sessions.

**Important notes for review:**

- The app does not require account creation or login.
- The app is fully functional offline. No internet connection is needed.
- All gameplay features are available immediately after launch.
- No in-app purchases, subscriptions, or advertisements are included in this version.
- No external purchases are required.
- User data (game history, settings, best score) is stored locally on device only using AsyncStorage. No data is transmitted to any server.
- The app is intended for general audiences as a casual educational math game.
- Privacy Policy is accessible from the Settings screen within the app.
- The app is iPhone-only (iPad is not supported).

Please let us know if any additional information is needed.
